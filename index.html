What is Matcha?

Matcha is a special kind of powdered green tea, traditionally used in the Japanese tea ceremony. Matcha is unique among other types of green tea in both appearance and taste. It has a vibrant, beautiful green color, a subtle aroma, and a rich umami taste. The benefits of matcha are far superior to other caffeinated beverages. It is known to be high in antioxidants, it strengthens your immune system, boosts energy levels, and detoxifies the body.
